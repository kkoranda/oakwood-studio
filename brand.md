# Oakwood Studio — Brand Guidelines

## Color Palette

### Primary
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Oak Dark | `#2c2a27` | Primary text, wordmark |
| Oakwood Warm | `#8b7355` | Accents, stem/wood tones, borders |

### Greens (Leaf)
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Deep Olive | `#4a5a3e` | Dark accent, vein details |
| Forest | `#5c6e4e` | Primary leaf, strong brand color |
| Olive | `#6b7f5a` | Secondary leaf, "STUDIO" wordmark |
| Sage | `#7a8e68` | Lighter leaf lobes, highlights |
| Mist | `#e8ece4` | Light background tint |

### Neutrals
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Stone 900 | `#1c1917` | Headings |
| Stone 700 | `#44403c` | Body text |
| Stone 500 | `#57534e` | Secondary text |
| Stone 200 | `#e7e5e4` | Borders, dividers |
| Stone 50 | `#fafaf9` | Page background |
| White | `#ffffff` | Card surfaces |

## Typography

**Primary:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: 600–700 weight
- Body: 400 weight
- Labels/caps: 500 weight, letter-spacing 1.5px

**Fallback:** -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif

## Logo Usage

### Full Logo (icon + wordmark)
- File: `logo.svg`
- Use on website header, proposals, and documents
- Minimum width: 200px
- "Oakwood" in Oak Dark (#2c2a27), 600 weight
- "STUDIO" in Olive (#6b7f5a), 400 weight, tracked wide

### Icon Mark
- File: `logo-mark.svg`
- Use as favicon, app icon, social avatar
- Minimum size: 32px
- Works on light and dark backgrounds

### Clear Space
- Maintain padding equal to the height of the "STUDIO" text on all sides

## Tone of Voice

- Confident but not boastful
- Technical but accessible
- Direct — lead with substance, not jargon
- Warm and grounded — we build things with care
- No buzzwords, no filler

## Brand Positioning

"Purpose-built software for the industries and people that design, assess, and manage the built environment."
